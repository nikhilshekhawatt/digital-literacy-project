While researching this topic, what surprised me the most was how common and easy cyber scams have become. I did not realize that even a simple message or QR code could lead to financial fraud. Many scams look very real, and it is easy for anyone to get tricked if they are not careful. I was also surprised to learn that students and young people are frequent targets because they use digital platforms regularly.

As a result, one habit I will personally change is being more careful before clicking on any links or approving any online payments. I will always verify the source of messages and double-check UPI requests before accepting them. I will also avoid sharing personal information online without thinking. This awareness will help me stay safer and make better decisions while using the internet.
