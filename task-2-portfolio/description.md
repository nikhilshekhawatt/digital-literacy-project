I chose three important platforms: LinkedIn, GitHub, and Stack Overflow, as they are highly useful for students in the technology field.

LinkedIn is mainly used for building a professional profile, connecting with industry experts, and exploring internships and job opportunities. GitHub is used to store, manage, and showcase coding projects, which helps in building a strong portfolio. Stack Overflow is a platform where developers ask and answer technical questions, making it a valuable resource for learning and problem-solving.

Over the next four years, I plan to actively use LinkedIn to grow my network, share achievements, and stay updated with industry trends. I will use GitHub to regularly upload my projects, collaborate with others, and improve my coding skills. Additionally, I will use Stack Overflow to clear my doubts, learn from experienced developers, and gradually start answering questions to contribute to the community.

These platforms will play a crucial role in my academic growth and career development.
